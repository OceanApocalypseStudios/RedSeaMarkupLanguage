# Why use RSML?
Now that you know what RSML is, you may be wondering: why should I use it?
Well, RSML has gained the trust of **over 2.4k project owners**, an impressive number for a markup language that is still in its early stages. You may be curious about what makes RSML stand out from other markup languages. Here are some of the key reasons why RSML is a great choice for your projects:

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   :material-source-branch:{ .lg .middle } **Logic-first design**

    ---
    RSML has been built from the ground up with logic in mind. Every feature, every syntax choice, and every design decision has been made with the goal of making RSML as logical and intuitive as possible. This means that when you use RSML, you can focus on what matters most: creating great content

    How many times, as a developer, have you been stuck with something that made no sense yet you had to use it? Well then, I have good news for you: RSML is here to change that.

-   :simple-dotnet:{ .lg .middle } **.NET Support**

    ---
    RSML has been written in C# from the very beginning. This means that its codebase is organized, easy to read, and easy to refactor. If it ever comes down to it, you can easily find solutions to issues that may arise by simply looking at RSML's code.
    
    Furthermore, RSML is designed to integrate seamlessly with .NET projects. Whether you're building a web application, a desktop application, or any other type of project using .NET, RSML can be easily incorporated into your workflow.

    Which begs the question: what are you waiting for? RSML is ready to be used in your .NET projects today. Just add (1) the RSML package to your project and start using it right away.
    { .annotate }

    1. `#!bash dotnet add package OceanApocalypseStudios.RSML`

-   :simple-rocket:{ .lg .middle } **Active Development and Ecosystem**

    ---
    RSML is under active development, with new features and improvements being added regularly. While still relatively new, RSML has already made a positive impact in an ecosystem dominated by older markup languages that needed modernization.

    By choosing RSML, you're saying 'yes' to a future-proof markup language; you're saying 'yes' to docuentation that helps; you're saying 'yes' to a language that has came to change things for the better.

    So why wait? Join the 2.4k+ project owners who have already embraced RSML and experience the benefits for yourself.

-   :simple-speedtest:{ .lg .middle } **Performance and Efficiency**

    ---
    RSML is designed to be lightweight and efficient, ensuring that your projects run smoothly without unnecessary overhead. Its optimized parsing and evaluating processes mean that you can embed it into your applications without worrying about performance bottlenecks.

    By using RSML, you're choosing a markup language that prioritizes speed and efficiency, allowing you to deliver a better experience to your users.

    And when it comes to users, every millisecond counts.

</div>
